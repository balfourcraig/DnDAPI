Message

  • You point your finger toward a creature within range and whisper a message. The target (and only the target) hears the message and can reply in a whisper that only you can hear.
  • You can cast this spell through solid objects if you are familiar with the target and know it is beyond the barrier. Magical silence, 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood blocks the spell. The spell doesn't have to follow a straight line and can travel freely around corners or through openings.

    General Tips:

    • Communicating silently with party members during a stealth mission or in a crowded area
    • Coordinating plans with party members when split up or exploring different areas
    • Sending secret messages to NPCs without others knowing
    • Possibly negotiating with enemies or prisoners through the spell
    • Asking for help or information from allies who are far away or in another location

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Communicating with a distant teammate during a stealth mission
    • Secretly relaying information to someone at a diplomatic meeting without being overheard
    • Hiding in a separate room during an interrogation and using Message to give your ally answers to the interrogator's questions
    • Coordinating a surprise attack on an enemy camp by whispering plans to your allies without alerting guards
    • Using Message to comfort someone from afar during a difficult emotional moment
