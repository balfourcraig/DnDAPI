Beacon of Hope

This spell bestows hope and vitality. Choose any number of creatures within range. For the duration, each target has advantage on wisdom saving throws and death saving throws, and regains the maximum number of hit points possible from any healing.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Before a tough battle to boost the party's confidence and increase their chances of success
    • During a difficult chase or escape to inspire hope and determination among the group
    • When negotiating with an enemy or other challenging situation to show that the party is strong and unified
    • When a party member is in danger or injured to give them a morale boost and aid in their recovery
    • When traveling through unfamiliar or dangerous territory to keep up morale and maintain a sense of optimism

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Healing a party member who has fallen unconscious in battle
    • Giving the party an extra boost of energy before a boss fight
    • Reviving a fallen NPC who is essential to completing a quest
    • Buffing a group of allies to face a powerful enemy army
    • Rallying the spirits of a town or village in need of morale
