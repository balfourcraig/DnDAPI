Jump

You touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Jump to reach high ledges or platforms that are out of reach
    • Jump over obstacles or gaps in the terrain
    • Jump to avoid traps or hazards on the ground
    • Jump to surprise enemies or gain distance in combat
    • Jump across bodies of water or over quicksand

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Jumping over a wide chasm or gap.
    • Jumping onto a floating platform that is out of reach.
    • Jumping onto the back of a flying creature to hitch a ride.
    • Jumping over obstacles or enemies in combat.
    • Jumping onto a higher ledge to gain a strategic advantage in battle.
