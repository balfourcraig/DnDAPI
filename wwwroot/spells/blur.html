Blur

Your body becomes blurred, shifting and wavering to all who can see you. For the duration, any creature has disadvantage on attack rolls against you. An attacker is immune to this effect if it doesn't rely on sight, as with blindsight, or can see through illusions, as with truesight.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Use Blur before entering a battle to increase your chances of evading enemy attacks.
    • Cast Blur while attempting to hide from enemies to make it more difficult for them to see you.
    • Use Blur when retreating from a battle to decrease the likelihood of being hit while running away.
    • Cast Blur while attempting to sneak past enemies to increase the chances of avoiding detection.
    • Use Blur when approaching a dangerous or unknown area to increase your chances of avoiding traps or ambushes.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Escaping from an enemy's line of sight.
    • Creating confusion by disguising the appearance of a party member or object.
    • Making it difficult for an enemy to hit a specific target in combat.
    • Making it easier to sneak by enemies by blending in with the surroundings.
    • Concealing a party member's identity during a negotiation or infiltration mission.
