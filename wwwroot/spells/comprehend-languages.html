Comprehend Languages

  • For the duration, you understand the literal meaning of any spoken language that you hear. You also understand any written language that you see, but you must be touching the surface on which the words are written. It takes about 1 minute to read one page of text.
  • This spell doesn't decode secret messages in a text or a glyph, such as an arcane sigil, that isn't part of a written language.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Understanding important clues written in a foreign language on a quest scroll
    • Eavesdropping on conversations in a language that the party doesn't speak
    • Reading ancient texts or inscriptions that have been written in an unfamiliar language
    • Deciphering spells and magic runes on a ritual circle or enchanted object
    • Interrogating prisoners who might not speak a language that the party knows

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Interpreting a foreign language in order to negotiate a peace treaty with a neighboring kingdom.
    • Deciphering an ancient text that holds the key to unlocking a powerful magical artifact.
    • Understanding the conversations of a group of enemies that you're spying on.
    • Reading an inscription in a dungeon that provides clues to the location of a hidden treasure.
    • Communicating with a race of intelligent creatures that don't speak Common.
