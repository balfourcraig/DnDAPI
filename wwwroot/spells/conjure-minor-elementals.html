Conjure Minor Elementals

  • You summon elementals that appear in unoccupied spaces that you can see within range. You choose one the following options for what appears:
  • - One elemental of challenge rating 2 or lower
  • - Two elementals of challenge rating 1 or lower
  • - Four elementals of challenge rating 1/2 or lower
  • - Eight elementals of challenge rating 1/4 or lower.
  • An elemental summoned by this spell disappears when it drops to 0 hit points or when the spell ends.
  • The summoned creatures are friendly to you and your companions. Roll initiative for the summoned creatures as a group, which has its own turns. They obey any verbal commands that you issue to them (no action required by you). If you don't issue any commands to them, they defend themselves from hostile creatures, but otherwise take no actions.
  • The GM has the creatures' statistics.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using certain higher-level spell slots, you choose one of the summoning options above, and more creatures appear: twice as many with a 6th-level slot and three times as many with an 8th-level slot.

General Tips:

  • Use Conjure Minor Elementals to summon creatures that can help with combat situations.
  • Summon elementals with specific traits that can help you in a particular environment or encounter.
  • Create temporary distractions to allow you to escape dangerous situations.
  • Use the elementals as a form of reconnaissance, sending them ahead to scout out an area.
  • Summon multiple elementals of lower CR to help with multiple tasks, such as scouting, combat, and solving puzzles.

Interesting Scenarios:

  • When you need extra protection during a battle, summon four sprites to aid you.
  • When you need to pass a difficult obstacle, summon two magmins to help you melt ice or rock.
  • When you need to distract an enemy, summon a single mephit to create chaos.
  • When you need to explore dark caves, summon two quasits to help you see in the dark.
  • When you need to create a distraction during a heist, summon four dust mephits to spread dust and smoke.
