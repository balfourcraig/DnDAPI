Magic Weapon

You touch a nonmagical weapon. Until the spell ends, that weapon becomes a magic weapon with a +1 bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls.

Spell Rules

  • 2
  • Transmutation
  • 1 bonus action
  • Touch
  • Up to 1 hour
    • V
    • S
    • Paladin
    • Wizard

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the bonus increases to +2. When you use a spell slot of 6th level or higher, the bonus increases to +3.

General Tips:

  • During combat encounters where enemies have resistances or immunities to non-magical attacks
  • In situations where weapon restrictions limit the amount of damage a character can deal
  • Against creatures with damage reduction abilities
  • In situations where the party is low on magical weapons and needs a quick boost in damage output
  • To overcome obstacles or barriers that require magical force to break through

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Defeating a powerful spellcaster who is resistant to physical attacks
  • Engaging in a battle against a horde of undead enemies
  • Exploring a dungeon with heavily armored enemies
  • Fighting a dragon with tough scales
  • Defending yourself against a powerful foe wielding a magical weapon
  • Surviving an encounter with an elemental creature that is immune to normal weapons
  • Solving a puzzle or completing a task that requires the use of a specific type of weapon
  • Assisting a party member who has lost or broken their own weapon
