Cure Wounds

A creature you touch regains a number of hit points equal to 1d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 1st.

General Tips:

  • During combat to heal an injured ally and keep them in the fight
  • After combat to heal wounds and prevent further damage in case of another skirmish
  • When exploring unknown territory to heal injuries from traps or dangerous creatures
  • When negotiating with hostile creatures to show good faith and prevent conflict
  • Before a big battle or boss fight to ensure all party members are in good health

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Healing a fallen comrade during combat to prevent their death
  • Reviving a character who has been knocked unconscious
  • Treating injuries sustained during battle
  • Healing wounds inflicted by traps or environmental hazards
  • Providing first aid to non-player characters in need (e.g. villagers, allies, etc.)
