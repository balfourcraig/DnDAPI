Transport via Plants

This spell creates a magical link between a Large or larger inanimate plant within range and another plant, at any distance, on the same plane of existence. You must have seen or touched the destination plant at least once before. For the duration, any creature can step into the target plant and exit from the destination plant by using 5 feet of movement.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Quickly travel long distances without the need for mounts or transportation
    • Avoid obstacles and barriers that would normally slow down or prevent travel
    • Sneak into and out of areas undetected, as the spell allows for travel through plants without harming them
    • Escape dangerous situations by melding into plants and passing unnoticed by enemies
    • Surprise enemies by appearing suddenly from a plant near their position
    • Gather intelligence by traveling through plant roots and listening in on conversations or observing hidden areas
    • Take cover and hide from enemies by melding into plants and becoming invisible

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • As a quick escape from danger by transporting yourself through a nearby tree to safety.
    • To quickly move yourself or your party through thick forest terrain without having to fight or maneuver around obstacles.
    • To retrieve an item that was lost or left behind in a dangerous location without having to physically go back.
    • To surprise or ambush an enemy by transporting yourself or your party through the ground or vegetation to get the drop on them.
    • To explore new or unknown areas by popping in and out of different plants to scout ahead or make a quick getaway.
