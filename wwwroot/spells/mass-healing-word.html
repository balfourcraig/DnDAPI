Mass Healing Word

As you call out words of restoration, up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range regain hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

Spell Rules

  • 3
  • Evocation
  • 1 bonus action
  • 60 feet
  • Instantaneous
  • V
  • Cleric

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 4th level or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 3rd.

General Tips:

  • During a boss fight to heal multiple party members at once
  • After taking heavy damage in a battle to quickly get everyone back to fighting shape
  • When low on resources and unable to use individual healing spells on every party member
  • When a group of allies or innocent NPCs need immediate healing
  • As a pre-emptive measure when entering a potentially dangerous area or encountering a particularly tough enemy

Interesting Scenarios:

  • After a fierce battle, the party is low on health and in need of a quick pick-me-up.
  • When facing a team of tough enemies, the Mass Healing Word can give the party an advantage in the fight.
  • In a tense situation where time is of the essence, using the spell can maximize healing efficiency.
  • If a character is knocked unconscious or has low hit points, using the Mass Healing Word can bring them back into the fight.
  • When traveling in dangerous territory, the spell can be used to ensure that the party stays healthy and ready for any obstacles they may face.
