Shield

An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from magic missile.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Use the Shield spell when you are about to be hit by an enemy attack.
    • Use the Shield spell to protect yourself from a surprise attack.
    • Use the Shield spell when you are low on hit points and cannot afford to take any damage.
    • Use the Shield spell to protect a teammate who is in danger of being hit.
    • Use the Shield spell to defend against spells that target you directly.
    • Use the Shield spell during a boss battle to avoid taking massive damage.
    • Use the Shield spell when you are being pursued by a dangerous enemy.
    • Use the Shield spell to save yourself or others from death in a critical moment.
