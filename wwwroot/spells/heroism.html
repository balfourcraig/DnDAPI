Heroism

A willing creature you touch is imbued with bravery. Until the spell ends, the creature is immune to being frightened and gains temporary hit points equal to your spellcasting ability modifier at the start of each of its turns. When the spell ends, the target loses any remaining temporary hit points from this spell.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Use Heroism to mitigate fear effects or to resist being frightened.
    • Chose a solo fighter or a tank and cast Heroism on them to raise their bravery and damage output in combat.
    • Use it on a character that tends to draw a lot of aggro, helping them stay in the fight longer.
    • Combine Heroism with other spells or effects that grant additional temporary hit points, for a massive boost to survivability.
    • Casting it on a character making skill checks during tense or important scenarios can provide an extra measure of confidence and courage.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • To save a party member from a deadly trap or ambush
    • To inspire allies in battle and give them the courage to fight on
    • To sacrifice yourself for the greater good and protect others from harm
    • To lead a charge and break through enemy lines, inspiring your allies to follow
    • To intimidate an opponent or group of enemies, making them back down or flee
