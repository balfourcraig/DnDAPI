Eldritch Blast

A beam of crackling energy streaks toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 force damage. The spell creates more than one beam when you reach higher levels: two beams at 5th level, three beams at 11th level, and four beams at 17th level. You can direct the beams at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each beam.

    General Tips:

    • Use Eldritch Blast as a ranged attack against enemies that are too far away to reach with melee attacks
    • Use Eldritch Blast to target enemies that are resistant or immune to certain types of damage
    • Use Eldritch Blast to disrupt spellcasting enemies, potentially interrupting their spells
    • Use Eldritch Blast to attack enemies that are hiding behind cover or in high positions
    • Use Eldritch Blast to weaken enemies with strong defenses, such as heavily armored foes
    • Use Eldritch Blast to knock enemies back, potentially moving them away from allies who are in danger
    • Use Eldritch Blast to deal consistent damage over time, especially when combined with the Agonizing Blast invocation
    • Use Eldritch Blast in combination with other spells or abilities, such as Hex or a Warlock's Pact Magic spells
