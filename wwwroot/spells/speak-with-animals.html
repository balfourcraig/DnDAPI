Speak with Animals

You gain the ability to comprehend and verbally communicate with beasts for the duration. The knowledge and awareness of many beasts is limited by their intelligence, but at a minimum, beasts can give you information about nearby locations and monsters, including whatever they can perceive or have perceived within the past day. You might be able to persuade a beast to perform a small favor for you, at the GM's discretion.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Talking to horses or other pack animals to negotiate easier travel
    • Communicating with guard dogs or other animals to navigate dangerous areas
    • Gathering information from local wildlife to aid in quests
    • Talking to familiars or animal companions to coordinate strategies in combat
    • Seeking advice from animal spirits or deities during religious ceremonies

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Communicating with a pack of wolves to gain information about nearby dangers
    • Speaking with a horse to negotiate safe passage through a dangerous terrain
    • Asking a flock of birds to scout ahead and report back on any potential threats
    • Communicating with a swarm of bees to learn the location of a hidden beehive filled with valuable honey
    • Trying to calm down a ferocious giant bear that is guarding the entrance to a treasure-filled cave
    • Asking a group of primates to distract a group of hostile enemies so you can sneak past undetected
    • Speaking with a family of rabbits to gather their knowledge of local terrain and resources
    • Talking to a group of curious dolphins to learn about underwater dangers and maybe even locate a lost sunken treasure
    • Asking a colony of ants to gather information about a nearby fortress and its defenses
    • Listening to the advice of an ancient tortoise who has lived in the area for centuries and knows all the secrets of the land
