Prayer of Healing

Up to six creatures of your choice that you can see within range each regain hit points equal to 2d8 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

Spell Rules

  • 2
  • Evocation
  • 10 minutes
  • 30 feet
  • Instantaneous
  • V
  • Cleric

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d8 for each slot level above 2nd.

General Tips:

  • During a boss battle where multiple party members may take damage
  • After a particularly challenging encounter to restore party health
  • When exploring deep dungeons or dangerous areas with little opportunity for rest
  • When facing a swarm of weaker enemies that may easily overwhelm the party
  • As a way to heal NPCs or allied creatures

Interesting Scenarios:

  • After a tough battle with a group of enemies, use Prayer of Healing to restore health to all injured party members.
  • If your party is taking a short rest, cast Prayer of Healing to help quickly recover hit points before moving on.
  • As a cleric, use Prayer of Healing to heal injured NPCs who may prove valuable allies later on.
  • In a raid against a highly fortified enemy stronghold, use Prayer of Healing to keep the entire party healthy and better prepared for what's to come.
  • During a heated boss battle, cast Prayer of Healing to ensure that your party stays alive long enough to defeat the enemy.
