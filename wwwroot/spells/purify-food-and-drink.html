Purify Food and Drink

All nonmagical food and drink within a 5-foot radius sphere centered on a point of your choice within range is purified and rendered free of poison and disease.

Spell Rules

  • 1
  • Transmutation
  • 1 action
  • 10 feet
  • Instantaneous
    • V
    • S
    • Cleric
    • Druid
    • Paladin

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • When foraging for food in an unfamiliar area
    • When drinking from a water source that may contain harmful bacteria or toxins
    • When unsure of the safety of purchased food or beverages
    • In areas with a high risk of foodborne illness or poisoning
    • When preparing meals for a group, to ensure food safety

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • On a long journey through the wilderness, you come across a murky stream that appears to be contaminated. You use Purify Food and Drink to ensure that you and your party have safe drinking water for the rest of your journey.
    • You are invited to a feast at the palace of a wealthy nobleman. However, you are hesitant to trust the food and drink provided by someone you don't know. You use Purify Food and Drink to ensure that the feast is safe for consumption.
    • You are trapped in a dungeon and running low on supplies. You come across a store room filled with stale bread and dirty water. You use Purify Food and Drink to make the food and drink safe to consume.
    • You stumble across a village whose water source has been poisoned by a malicious cult. You use Purify Food and Drink to cleanse the water and save the village from illness and death.
    • You are traveling through a desert and find some juicy-looking fruits growing from a cactus. You use Purify Food and Drink to ensure that the fruit is safe to eat before indulging.
