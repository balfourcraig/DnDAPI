Magic Missile

You create three glowing darts of magical force. Each dart hits a creature of your choice that you can see within range. A dart deals 1d4 + 1 force damage to its target. The darts all strike simultaneously, and you can direct them to hit one creature or several.

Spell Rules

  • 1
  • Evocation
  • 1 action
  • 120 feet
  • Instantaneous
    • V

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the spell creates one more dart for each slot level above 1st.

General Tips:

  • Use Magic Missile to target enemy spellcasters, as it always hits and can disrupt their concentration.
  • When dealing with multiple weak enemies, use Magic Missile to mop them up and conserve stronger spells for tougher foes.
  • During a surprise encounter, use Magic Missile as a first strike to deal damage before the enemy can react.
  • Combine Magic Missile with a spell that creates darkness or obscures vision, such as Fog Cloud, to make it more difficult for enemies to dodge the incoming missiles.
  • Use Magic Missile to finish off an enemy that is near death but out of reach of melee attacks.

Interesting Scenarios:

  • When you need to attack a target that is far away and difficult to reach
  • When you want to bypass an enemy's armor or defenses
  • When you need to deal damage quickly and efficiently
  • When you are facing multiple enemies at once and need to spread your damage
  • When you are facing a particularly tough enemy and need to bring them down quickly
  • When you need to protect yourself or a teammate with a spell that never misses its target
  • When you want to show off your magical prowess in front of your party members
