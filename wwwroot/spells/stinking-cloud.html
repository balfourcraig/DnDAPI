Stinking Cloud

  • You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of yellow, nauseating gas centered on a point within range. The cloud spreads around corners, and its area is heavily obscured. The cloud lingers in the air for the duration.
  • Each creature that is completely within the cloud at the start of its turn must make a constitution saving throw against poison. On a failed save, the creature spends its action that turn retching and reeling. Creatures that don't need to breathe or are immune to poison automatically succeed on this saving throw.
  • A moderate wind (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses the cloud after 4 rounds. A strong wind (at least 20 miles per hour) disperses it after 1 round.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Use Stinking Cloud to create a barrier between enemies and allies to limit movement and create space to safely retreat.
    • Use Stinking Cloud in a choke point or narrow hallway to limit enemy movement and force them to take damage or become incapacitated.
    • Use Stinking Cloud to distract enemies, creating an opportunity to attack or flank them.
    • Use Stinking Cloud to set up traps, forcing enemies into a specific area where they can be easily surrounded and attacked.
    • Use Stinking Cloud to disrupt spell casting or archery attacks, making it more difficult for enemies to hit you or your allies with ranged attacks.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Creating a diversion to distract enemies while your party sneaks past undetected.
    • Using it to disorient and incapacitate a group of enemies while your party takes them out one by one.
    • Blocking the entrance or exit of a room or building to prevent enemies from escaping or pursuing you.
    • Causing chaos and panicking a town or city by releasing the spell in a heavily populated area.
    • Casting the spell on a group of enemies who are all clustered together, causing them to disperse and making them easier to defeat.
