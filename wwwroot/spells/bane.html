Bane

Up to three creatures of your choice that you can see within range must make charisma saving throws. Whenever a target that fails this saving throw makes an attack roll or a saving throw before the spell ends, the target must roll a d4 and subtract the number rolled from the attack roll or saving throw.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 1st.

General Tips:

  • Use Bane on groups of enemies with high attack bonuses to reduce their chances to hit your party.
  • Combine Bane with other debuff spells, such as Slow or Darkness, to hinder your enemies' abilities even further.
  • Bane can also be effective in PvP situations, reducing the opponent's rolls and giving you an edge in combat.
  • If you have the War Caster feat, use Bane as an opportunity attack when an enemy tries to move away from you, reducing their future attacks against you.
  • Cast Bane before engaging in a tough battle to give your party an extra edge and increase your chances of success.

Interesting Scenarios:

  • To weaken a powerful enemy before engaging in a difficult battle
  • To reduce the effectiveness of a spellcaster's magic in combat
  • To increase the odds of success in a contested skill check or saving throw
  • To give a disadvantage to an opponent in negotiations or social interactions
  • To punish an enemy who has caused harm to the player or their allies
