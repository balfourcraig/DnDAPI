Spare the Dying

You touch a living creature that has 0 hit points. The creature becomes stable. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Use Spare the Dying to stabilize a fellow party member who has fallen unconscious during combat.
    • Cast Spare the Dying on an NPC or ally who has been mortally wounded in a quest, allowing them to live long enough to complete their task or reveal vital information.
    • Use Spare the Dying to give yourself an advantage in combat by keeping your party members alive and fighting.
    • Cast Spare the Dying on a fallen enemy to prevent them from dying, allowing for potential interrogation or trade for ransom.
    • Use Spare the Dying to extend the lifespan of group members who are suffering from a poison or disease, allowing them more time to find a cure.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • As a healer, when a fallen ally is beyond the reach of any other healing spell
    • During a boss fight, to prevent the enemy from getting a kill and gaining a morale advantage
    • When the party has limited resources and cannot afford to expend healing spells or potions on a downed ally
    • If a character has taken a significant amount of damage and is in danger of dying from one hit, using this spell as a precautionary measure
    • During a stealth mission, to silently revive a knocked out guard and question them without alerting others
