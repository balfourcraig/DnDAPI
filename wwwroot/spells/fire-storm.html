Fire Storm

  • A storm made up of sheets of roaring flame appears in a location you choose within range. The area of the storm consists of up to ten 10-foot cubes, which you can arrange as you wish. Each cube must have at least one face adjacent to the face of another cube. Each creature in the area must make a dexterity saving throw. It takes 7d10 fire damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
  • The fire damages objects in the area and ignites flammable objects that aren't being worn or carried. If you choose, plant life in the area is unaffected by this spell.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • When facing a group of enemies that are clustered tightly together, Fire Storm can deal massive damage to all of them at once.
    • In situations where your party needs to take down a large, powerful enemy quickly, using Fire Storm can help to whittle away its health.
    • If you find yourself surrounded and outnumbered, casting Fire Storm can help to clear a path for you to escape.
    • It can be useful in siege situations where you need to take out a group of enemies that are huddled behind barricades or fortifications.
    • If you're trapped in a confined space and need to create some breathing room, you can use Fire Storm to force enemies to scatter and give you time to maneuver.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Defending against a group of enemies charging towards you
    • Battling a horde of undead creatures
    • Clearing out a heavily guarded fortress
    • Intimidating a group of enemies with a powerful display of magic
    • Setting a distraction or diversion for a stealthy mission

    How do you want to do this?:

    • A burst of flames shoots out from your hands and engulfs your enemy, incinerating them instantly.
    • You gather flames from the environment and concentrate them into a blazing inferno that engulfs your enemy.
    • The air around your enemy heats up rapidly as flames burst out from under their feet and consume them.
    • A massive explosion of fire erupts from your hands, completely obliterating your enemy.
    • You raise your hands to the sky and call down a column of fire, which incinerates your enemy in a fiery inferno.
