Goodberry

  • Up to ten berries appear in your hand and are infused with magic for the duration. A creature can use its action to eat one berry. Eating a berry restores 1 hit point, and the berry provides enough nourishment to sustain a creature for a day.
  • The berries lose their potency if they have not been consumed within 24 hours of the casting of this spell.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Before a dangerous encounter, to top off everyone's health.
    • During a long trek through the wilderness, to keep the party fed and healthy without worrying about rations.
    • As a source of emergency healing, to save a downed ally or prevent them from dying outright.
    • To bolster the health of a particular party member who might be more vulnerable than the others.
    • To heal NPCs or animals that the party is trying to help or interact with.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • During a long and arduous journey through treacherous terrain, the Goodberry spell can provide sustenance and energy for you and your party.
    • If your party is low on resources and unable to rest, Goodberry can be a lifesaver by providing enough sustenance to allow you to keep moving.
    • In a stealth mission where engaging in combat would be detrimental, Goodberry can be used to heal injured party members without drawing unwanted attention.
    • If you need to barter with merchants or locals for information or services, Goodberry can be a valuable trade item in exchange for goods or services.
    • In a survival-themed campaign, where finding food and resources is difficult, the Goodberry spell can ensure that your party stays fed and healthy.
