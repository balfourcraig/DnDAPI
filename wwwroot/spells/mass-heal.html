Mass Heal

A flood of healing energy flows from you into injured creatures around you. You restore up to 700 hit points, divided as you choose among any number of creatures that you can see within range. Creatures healed by this spell are also cured of all diseases and any effect making them blinded or deafened. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • In the middle of a tough boss fight to revive multiple fallen allies
    • After a long rest to top off the party's health before heading into unknown territory
    • As a last resort to prevent a TPK (Total Party Kill) when things are looking grim
    • During a siege on a town or outpost to heal a large number of injured civilians or soldiers
    • In a battle against undead enemies, as Mass Heal also damages them

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • In a boss battle where multiple party members are low on health
    • After a party member triggers a trap that causes damage to everyone in the room
    • To quickly heal a group of NPCs that are vital to completing a quest
    • During a siege on a city where many soldiers and civilians are wounded
    • When facing a powerful undead enemy that deals damage to multiple targets at once
