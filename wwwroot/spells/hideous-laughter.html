Hideous Laughter

  • A creature of your choice that you can see within range perceives everything as hilariously funny and falls into fits of laughter if this spell affects it. The target must succeed on a wisdom saving throw or fall prone, becoming incapacitated and unable to stand up for the duration. A creature with an Intelligence score of 4 or less isn't affected.
  • At the end of each of its turns, and each time it takes damage, the target can make another wisdom saving throw. The target had advantage on the saving throw if it's triggered by damage. On a success, the spell ends.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • During a battle with enemies to disable them and make them easier targets for your team.
    • As a distraction to confuse or divert the attention of guards or other obstacles.
    • To break the tension in a tense scene, such as when negotiating with a stubborn NPC.
    • To humiliate a particularly arrogant or boastful villain, allowing you to gain the upper hand in negotiations or combat.
    • To cause a distraction in a crowded area, allowing you to slip by unnoticed or steal an item.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Tricking a guard into laughing uncontrollably, allowing the party to sneak past undetected.
    • Using the spell on a powerful enemy to give the party the upper hand in combat.
    • Humiliating a pompous noble or rival by making them burst into laughter during a public speech or event.
    • Causing a distracted librarian or researcher to burst into laughter, allowing the party to steal or copy important documents unnoticed.
    • Using the spell on a terrified or resistant NPC in order to extract information or gain their cooperation.
