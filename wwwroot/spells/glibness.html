Glibness

Until the spell ends, when you make a Charisma check, you can replace the number you roll with a 15. Additionally, no matter what you say, magic that would determine if you are telling the truth indicates that you are being truthful.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • During negotiations with NPCs to persuade them to lower prices, give you information, or agree to your terms
    • When trying to deceive or lie to NPCs, such as when trying to infiltrate an enemy stronghold or conning someone out of valuable information
    • During social encounters to impress or charm others, potentially gaining important allies or making new friends
    • When trying to convince NPCs to change their beliefs or opinions, potentially avoiding conflict or even changing the course of a quest or campaign
    • During roleplaying situations where you need to quickly improvise clever responses or come up with witty quips

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Convincing a strict guard to let the party enter a forbidden area.
    • Persuading a hostile NPC to reveal valuable information.
    • Swindling a merchant into selling goods at a much lower price.
    • Getting out of trouble with local law enforcement by smoothly talking your way out of a situation.
    • Convincing a group of villagers to aid the party in their quest.
