Wall of Thorns

  • You create a wall of tough, pliable, tangled brush bristling with needle-sharp thorns. The wall appears within range on a solid surface and lasts for the duration. You choose to make the wall up to 60 feet long, 10 feet high, and 5 feet thick or a circle that has a 20-foot diameter and is up to 20 feet high and 5 feet thick. The wall blocks line of sight.
  • When the wall appears, each creature within its area must make a dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes 7d8 piercing damage, or half as much damage on a successful save.
  • A creature can move through the wall, albeit slowly and painfully. For every 1 foot a creature moves through the wall, it must spend 4 feet of movement. Furthermore, the first time a creature enters the wall on a turn or ends its turn there, the creature must make a dexterity saving throw. It takes 7d8 slashing damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, both types of damage increase by 1d8 for each slot level above 6th.

General Tips:

  • Use the Wall of Thorns in a chokepoint to prevent enemies from advancing.
  • Place the wall around an enemy caster to prevent them from casting spells or moving.
  • Use the wall as a distraction to draw enemies towards it while your teammates flank them.
  • Place the wall in front of archers or ranged attackers to limit their line of sight and reduce their effectiveness.
  • Use the wall to block off an escape route for fleeing enemies.

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Defending a castle or stronghold against invading enemies
  • Creating a barrier around a village that is being attacked by monsters
  • Creating a barrier around campsite to keep dangerous wild animals away
  • Blocking off a path to prevent enemies from reaching an important location
  • Using it as a trap against enemies who are pursuing you

How do you want to do this?:

  • A wall of thorns erupts from the ground, impaling your enemy and dealing massive piercing damage.
  • The thorns wrap around your enemies, suffocating them and dealing ongoing damage until they break free.
  • A massive wave of thorns crashes down on your enemy, crushing them and dealing massive bludgeoning damage.
  • The wall of thorns engulfs your enemy in flames, dealing fire damage and leaving them with a painful burn.
  • Your enemy becomes entangled in the thorns, and they are left helpless and vulnerable to further attacks.
