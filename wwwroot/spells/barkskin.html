Barkskin

You touch a willing creature. Until the spell ends, the target's skin has a rough, bark-like appearance, and the target's AC can't be less than 16, regardless of what kind of armor it is wearing.

Spell Rules

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Before entering combat with a powerful enemy or boss
    • While exploring dangerous wilderness areas where ambushes are likely
    • When defending against an enemy caster who often uses spells that target your AC
    • When guarding a vulnerable creature or NPC who is traveling with your party
    • During siege or defense situations when enemy archers are raining arrows down on your party

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Camping in the wilderness and wanting extra protection from potential attacks from wild animals
    • Exploring deep in a dungeon with limited supplies and needing an extra layer of defense against traps and enemies
    • Preparing for a battle with a particularly deadly opponent, such as a dragon or demon
    • Confronting a group of bandits or enemy soldiers and needing to withstand their attacks
    • Attempting to sneak past guards or other obstacles and wanting added protection in case of detection
