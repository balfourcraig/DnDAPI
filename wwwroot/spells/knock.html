Knock

  • Choose an object that you can see within range. The object can be a door, a box, a chest, a set of manacles, a padlock, or another object that contains a mundane or magical means that prevents access.
  • A target that is held shut by a mundane lock or that is stuck or barred becomes unlocked, unstuck, or unbarred. If the object has multiple locks, only one of them is unlocked.
  • If you choose a target that is held shut with arcane lock, that spell is suppressed for 10 minutes, during which time the target can be opened and shut normally.
  • When you cast the spell, a loud knock, audible from as far away as 300 feet, emanates from the target object.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Knocking on locked doors to gain entry without breaking them
    • To alert someone on the other side of a door or wall quietly
    • To distract or confuse enemies in combat
    • To test if an object is hollow or not
    • To create a knocking rhythm as a signal to allies
    • To make a dramatic entrance by knocking on the door before bursting in

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Sneaking past a guard by knocking on a nearby surface to create a distraction.
    • Attempting to gain entry into a locked room or building by knocking on the door with the Knock spell.
    • Asking a secret password to access a hidden location with a Knock spell that only opens the door for those who know the password.
    • Using the Knock spell to disrupt the concentration of an enemy spellcaster during combat.
    • Creating a surprise entrance by knocking down a weakened door with the Knock spell.
