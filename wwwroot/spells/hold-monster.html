Hold Monster

Choose a creature you can see and reach. The target must make a saving throw of Wisdom or be paralyzed for the duration of the spell. This spell has no effect against the undead. At the end of each round, the target can make a new saving throw of Wisdom. If successful, the spell ends for the creature.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a level 6 or higher location, you can target an additional creature for each level of location beyond the fifth. The creatures must be within 30 feet o f each other when you target them.

General Tips:

  • Use Hold Monster to immobilize powerful enemies during combat so that your party can take them down one by one
  • Use Hold Monster to stop enemies from fleeing the battlefield, buying your party some time to regroup or finish off other foes
  • Use Hold Monster to hold a sentry in place while your party sneaks past undetected
  • Use Hold Monster to incapacitate a dangerous foe while you negotiate with them or gather important information
  • Use Hold Monster as an emergency escape tactic to prevent an enemy from attacking you as you flee from danger

Interesting Scenarios:

  • To disable an enemy spellcaster in a boss fight
  • To immobilize a powerful monster so the party can escape or prepare for battle
  • To prevent a fleeing enemy from getting away
  • To hold a hostile NPC still for questioning or interrogation
  • To protect a weakened party member by holding back an advancing horde of enemies
