Create Food and Water

You create 45 pounds of food and 30 gallons of water on the ground or in containers within range, enough to sustain up to fifteen humanoids or five steeds for 24 hours. The food is bland but nourishing, and spoils if uneaten after 24 hours. The water is clean and doesn't go bad.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • During a long journey or exploration, to ensure that your party always has enough food and water
    • In a dungeon or other dangerous location where finding food and water might be difficult
    • When camping or resting outdoors, to avoid having to spend time hunting, gathering or purifying water
    • To help out local towns or villages in times of drought or famine
    • During a battle or siege, to feed allies and bolster morale
    • In preparation for a long-term spellcasting ritual or other activity that requires sustained energy and hydration

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • During a long journey where food and water are scarce
    • In an underground dungeon where there are no available sources of food or water
    • In a barren, desert terrain where food and water are difficult to come by
    • During a siege or long-lasting battle where supplies are running low
    • As a distraction or bargaining tool when negotiating with enemies or allies
