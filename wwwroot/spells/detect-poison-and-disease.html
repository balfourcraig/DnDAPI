Detect Poison and Disease

  • For the duration, you can sense the presence and location of poisons, poisonous creatures, and diseases within 30 feet of you. You also identify the kind of poison, poisonous creature, or disease in each case.
  • The spell can penetrate most barriers, but it is blocked by 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood or dirt.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • When exploring a new dungeon or labyrinth, use Detect Poison and Disease to avoid contamination from poisonous or diseased substances.
    • Use it to detect poison in food or drink before consuming it.
    • When dealing with suspicious or untrustworthy characters, use Detect Poison and Disease to check for any foul play.
    • In battle, use the spell to locate and identify enemies that may be poisoned or infected with a disease, giving your party an edge in combat.
    • When investigating a crime scene, use Detect Poison and Disease to find clues and identify potential suspects.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Investigating a murder case and checking if any of the food or drink at the crime scene has been poisoned.
    • Preparing for battle and checking if your weapons or armor are contaminated with a harmful disease.
    • Exploring a dungeon and checking for poisonous traps or environmental hazards.
    • Meeting a suspicious NPC and checking if they are attempting to poison you or are infected with a deadly disease.
    • Searching for rare herbs or ingredients and checking if they are safe to consume or use in potions.
