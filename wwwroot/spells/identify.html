Identify

  • You choose one object that you must touch throughout the casting of the spell. If it is a magic item or some other magic-imbued object, you learn its properties and how to use them, whether it requires attunement to use, and how many charges it has, if any. You learn whether any spells are affecting the item and what they are. If the item was created by a spell, you learn which spell created it.
  • If you instead touch a creature throughout the casting, you learn what spells, if any, are currently affecting it.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • When encountering a disguised enemy
    • To identify the properties of magical items
    • To determine if a creature is under the influence of a curse or other magical effect
    • To discern the true nature or identity of a shape-shifted or illusioned creature
    • To determine if an object or creature is of a particular type or origin

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Identifying magical items found in a treasure chest.
    • Discovering the source and purpose of a mysterious item obtained during a quest.
    • Determining the identity and intentions of a disguised creature, such as a doppleganger or changeling.
    • Confirming the authenticity of a spell scroll before attempting to cast it.
    • Uncovering the weaknesses and vulnerabilities of a powerful enemy or boss monster.
    • Verifying the magical properties and effects of a potion or elixir.
    • Identifying the type and strength of a magical trap that has been triggered.
    • Pinpointing the location of a hidden or concealed magical object.
    • Revealing the true form and abilities of an illusion or glamor magic.
