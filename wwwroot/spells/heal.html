Heal

Choose a creature that you can see within range. A surge of positive energy washes through the creature, causing it to regain 70 hit points. This spell also ends blindness, deafness, and any diseases affecting the target. This spell has no effect on constructs or undead.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 7th level or higher, the amount of healing increases by 10 for each slot level above 6th.

General Tips:

  • During combat to restore a wounded ally's hit points and keep them in the fight
  • After combat to heal injuries and avoid the need for extended rest
  • In social situations to impress NPCs with your healing abilities and gain their favor
  • During exploration to heal injuries sustained from traps or dangerous terrain
  • In emergency situations to help stabilize a dying ally and prevent death saves

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Reviving a fallen ally who is knocked unconscious during battle
  • Healing a fellow party member who has been poisoned or injured by traps or environmental hazards during exploration
  • Curing a disease that has afflicted a member of the party or an NPC encountered during the adventure
  • Restoring hit points and curing injuries that have been inflicted by enemies or environmental factors during combat
  • Improving the overall health and well-being of the party after a grueling battle or extended period of travel
