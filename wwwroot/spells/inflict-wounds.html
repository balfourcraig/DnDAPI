Inflict Wounds

Make a melee spell attack against a creature you can reach. On a hit, the target takes 3d10 necrotic damage.

Spell Rules

  • 1
  • Necromancy
  • 1 action
  • Touch
  • Instantaneous
    • V
    • S
  • Cleric

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the damage increases by 1d10 for each slot level above 1st.

General Tips:

  • When facing a tough enemy in combat
  • When your allies are in danger and need backup damage
  • When trying to intimidate or scare NPCs
  • When attempting to interrogate a prisoner
  • When confronted with undead enemies, as the spell deals extra damage to them

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Defending against a surprise attack from an enemy
  • Trying to take down a powerful boss quickly
  • Interrupting an enemy spellcaster during a ritual or incantation
  • Getting revenge against a villain who harmed your character or their loved ones
  • As a last resort when all other spells and abilities have been exhausted
