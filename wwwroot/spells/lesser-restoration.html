Lesser Restoration

You touch a creature and can end either one disease or one condition afflicting it. The condition can be blinded, deafened, paralyzed, or poisoned.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Clearing a poisoned condition
    • Ending an effect that reduces an ability score, such as a spell or poison
    • Removing a disease
    • Stopping the effects of paralysis or petrification
    • Ending a curse or magic effect that affects the player's body

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Restoring a teammate's paralyzed limb so they can fight in battle
    • Removing a curse placed on a party member by an enemy mage
    • Healing a party member's exhaustion so they can continue on a long quest
    • Reviving a teammate from being stunned by a powerful foe
    • Removing a poison that a party member ingested while exploring a dangerous dungeon
