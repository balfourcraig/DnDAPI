Aid

Your spell bolsters your allies with toughness and resolve. Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's hit point maximum and current hit points increase by 5 for the duration.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, a target's hit points increase by an additional 5 for each slot level above 2nd.

General Tips:

  • During combat to heal injured allies
  • To stabilize a dying teammate
  • In a social situation to give advantage on a charisma-based skill check
  • To help a friend break free from a grapple or other restrained condition
  • To support a teammate in a skill check, such as picking a lock or disarming a trap

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Healing an injured party member in the middle of a battle
  • Assisting a teammate in a challenging skill check
  • Providing aid to a non-combatant NPC in need
  • Helping lift heavy objects or move obstacles out of the way
  • Boosting a party member's attack roll or saving throw during combat
