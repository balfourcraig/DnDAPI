Find Steed

  • You summon a spirit that assumes the form of an unusually intelligent, strong, and loyal steed, creating a long-lasting bond with it. Appearing in an unoccupied space within range, the steed takes on a form that you choose, such as a warhorse, a pony, a camel, an elk, or a mastiff. (Your GM might allow other animals to be summoned as steeds.) The steed has the statistics of the chosen form, though it is a celestial, fey, or fiend (your choice) instead of its normal type. Additionally, if your steed has an Intelligence of 5 or less, its Intelligence becomes 6, and it gains the ability to understand one language of your choice that you speak.
  • Your steed serves you as a mount, both in combat and out, and you have an instinctive bond with it that allows you to fight as a seamless unit. While mounted on your steed, you can make any spell you cast that targets only you also target your steed.
  • When the steed drops to 0 hit points, it disappears, leaving behind no physical form. You can also dismiss your steed at any time as an action, causing it to disappear. In either case, casting this spell again summons the same steed, restored to its hit point maximum.
  • While your steed is within 1 mile of you, you can communicate with it telepathically.
  • You can't have more than one steed bonded by this spell at a time. As an action, you can release the steed from its bond at any time, causing it to disappear.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Exploring a new area: Your steed can help you cover large distances quickly and can also provide protection and mobility in combat.
    • Scouting and reconnaissance: A fast and agile steed can help you gather information on the terrain and the enemy's movements.
    • Transporting goods and equipment: Your steed can carry your backpack, weapons, and other items, freeing you up to focus on other tasks.
    • Assaulting heavily guarded fortifications: A heavily armored steed can help you break through enemy lines and reach the target quickly.
    • Retreating from danger: Your steed can greatly increase your chances of survival if you need to make a quick escape from a dangerous situation.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • As a paladin traveling through rough terrain, you cast Find Steed to summon a sturdy warhorse to help carry your equipment.
    • During a dungeon crawl, you use Find Steed to summon a large wolf to track down hidden enemies and detect traps.
    • As a holy knight, you use Find Steed to summon a majestic unicorn to aid in your quest to defeat a dark necromancer.
    • In a battle against goblins, you cast Find Steed to summon a nimble riding dog to weave in and out of combat, harassing your opponents.
    • During a diplomatic meeting with a group of centaurs, you use Find Steed to summon a graceful horse to show respect and establish trust.
