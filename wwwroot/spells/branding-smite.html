Branding Smite

The next time you hit a creature with a weapon attack before this spell ends, the weapon gleams with astral radiance as you strike. The attack deals an extra 2d6 radiant damage to the target, which becomes visible if it's invisible, and the target sheds dim light in a 5-foot radius and can't become invisible until the spell ends.

Spell Rules

  • 2
  • Evocation
  • 1 bonus action
  • Self
  • Up to 1 minute
  • V
  • Paladin

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, the extra damage increases by 1d6 for each slot level above 2nd.

General Tips:

  • Use Branding Smite when facing enemies with resistance or immunity to non-magical attacks.
  • Use Branding Smite when fighting undead or fiends, as it deals extra damage to these creatures.
  • Save Branding Smite for critical moments, such as when facing a boss or in a dire situation.
  • Pair Branding Smite with spells or abilities that grant advantage on attack rolls, such as Bless or True Strike.
  • Use Branding Smite before casting a spell that requires concentration, as it does not require concentration itself.

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Defending your personal brand against a rival in a business setting
  • Branding the weapons and armor of your party to strike fear into your enemies
  • Using the spell on a creature that has a recognizable brand/logo to weaken their authority
  • Branding an important document or seal to ensure its authenticity and deter forgery
  • Punishing a villain who has misused and tarnished a respected brand or family crest

How do you want to do this?:

  • The Branding Smite spell imbues the fighter's weapon with divine energy.
  • The next time the fighter lands a hit with their weapon, the target will be engulfed in flames.
  • The flames that surround the target will deal additional radiant damage on top of the normal weapon damage.
  • The radiant damage from the Branding Smite spell will remain with the target, visibly marking them and making them easier to track.
  • The mark from the Branding Smite spell can only be removed with a successful Dispel Magic spell or after a long rest.
