Fog Cloud

You create a 20-foot-radius sphere of fog centered on a point within range. The sphere spreads around corners, and its area is heavily obscured. It lasts for the duration or until a wind of moderate or greater speed (at least 10 miles per hour) disperses it.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the radius of the fog increases by 20 feet for each slot level above 1st.

General Tips:

  • Use the Fog Cloud spell to obscure your party's movements during a stealth mission.
  • Use the Fog Cloud spell to create cover in an open battlefield, making it harder for enemies to hit your party with ranged attacks.
  • Use the Fog Cloud spell to create a distraction, allowing your party to slip past guards or sneak into a guarded area.
  • Use the Fog Cloud spell to conceal traps or surprise attacks, giving your party an advantage during combat.
  • Use the Fog Cloud spell to block the view of enemies with darkvision, disrupting their ability to detect your party.
