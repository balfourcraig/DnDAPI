Create or Destroy Water

  • You either create or destroy water.
  • ***Create Water.*** You create up to 10 gallons of clean water within range in an open container. Alternatively, the water falls as rain in a 30-foot cube within range.
  • ***Destroy Water.*** You destroy up to 10 gallons of water in an open container within range. Alternatively, you destroy fog in a 30-foot cube within range.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, you create or destroy 10 additional gallons of water, or the size of the cube increases by 5 feet, for each slot level above 1st.

General Tips:

  • Put out large fires or create small rain showers to extinguish small flames
  • Create a source of drinking water when in a desert or other dry environment
  • Fill up a moat or other trench filled with water to make it more difficult for enemies to cross
  • Destroy water in a puddle or small pond to reveal any hidden objects or creatures lurking beneath the surface
  • Create a barrier of ice by freezing water to create a makeshift wall or bridge
  • Destroy water in a water-filled dungeon room to create a dry passage or floor to walk on
  • Create a makeshift water slide to slide down a steep hill or slope
  • Destroy water in a water-filled pit trap to escape if you fall in
  • Create a giant wave to push enemies back or knock them prone
  • Destroy water in an enemy's water supply to leave them without a source of hydration

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Surviving in the desert by creating water to drink and using it to irrigate crops
  • Clearing a path through a flooded dungeon by destroying excess water
  • Putting out a raging fire by creating enough water to douse the flames
  • Filling a dried-up well or reservoir with created water to provide a town with drinking water
  • Creating a large body of water to create a natural barrier or obstacle for enemies to cross
