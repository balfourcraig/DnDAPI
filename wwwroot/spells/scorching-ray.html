Scorching Ray

  • You create three rays of fire and hurl them at targets within range. You can hurl them at one target or several.
  • Make a ranged spell attack for each ray. On a hit, the target takes 2d6 fire damage.

Spell Rules

  • 2
  • Evocation
  • 1 action
  • 120 feet
  • Instantaneous
    • V
    • S
    • Sorcerer
    • Wizard

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 3rd level or higher, you create one additional ray for each slot level above 2nd.

General Tips:

  • Use Scorching Ray to deal damage to enemies in combat.
  • Target multiple enemies with Scorching Ray to maximize its effectiveness.
  • Use Scorching Ray to set objects or structures on fire.
  • Scorching Ray can be used to create a diversion or distraction in a battle.
  • When facing enemies that are resistant to certain types of spell damage, use Scorching Ray's fire damage to bypass their resistance.

Interesting Scenarios:

  • To attack a group of enemies clustered together
  • To melt through a locked door or obstacle
  • To set fire to flammable objects or structures
  • To intimidate or scare off a group of adversaries
  • To create a diversion or distraction in a battle
