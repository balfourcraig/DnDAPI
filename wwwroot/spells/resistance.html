Resistance

You touch one willing creature. Once before the spell ends, the target can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to one saving throw of its choice. It can roll the die before or after making the saving throw. The spell then ends.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • In combat situations where you may be targeted with elemental or magical attacks
    • When crossing hazardous terrain such as icy mountains or scorching deserts
    • To help non-magical party members resist harmful effects from spells or poisons
    • During negotiations or social encounters where an opponent may be using mind-altering spells or abilities
    • To protect against traps that trigger magical effects or elemental damage

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Resisting mind-altering spells or effects from enemy spellcasters
    • Breaking free from physical restraints or bindings
    • Withstanding the effects of extreme weather or environmental hazards
    • Protecting yourself or allies from powerful elemental attacks
    • Overcoming fear or intimidation from enemies or frightening creatures
