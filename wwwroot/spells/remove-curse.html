Remove Curse

At your touch, all curses affecting one creature or object end. If the object is a cursed magic item, its curse remains, but the spell breaks its owner's attunement to the object so it can be removed or discarded.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Remove a curse from a cursed item, such as a weapon or piece of armor
    • Remove a curse from a teammate who has been cursed by a spell or cursed object
    • Remove a curse from an NPC who is under the influence of a curse, allowing them to provide important information or aid to the party
    • Remove a curse from a location, such as a haunted house or cursed graveyard, to lift a curse on the area and free trapped souls
    • Remove a curse from oneself, if the player character has been cursed by a powerful enemy or object

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Casting Remove Curse to release a cursed NPC from a powerful enchantment
    • Removing a hex on a valuable artifact to allow it to be used safely
    • Breaking a curse on a player character's family member or friend, causing them to return to their normal self
    • Undoing a curse placed by a vengeful witch on a whole town or village
    • Using Remove Curse to clear a haunted location to prevent future curses on anyone who enters
