Stone Shape

You touch a stone object of Medium size or smaller or a section of stone no more than 5 feet in any dimension and form it into any shape that suits your purpose. So, for example, you could shape a large rock into a weapon, idol, or coffer, or make a small passage through a wall, as long as the wall is less than 5 feet thick. You could also shape a stone door or its frame to seal the door shut. The object you create can have up to two hinges and a latch, but finer mechanical detail isn't possible.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Create a secret door in a stone wall to sneak into a fortress.
    • Smooth out rough terrain to make it easier to traverse.
    • Create a bridge over a small stream or gap.
    • Repair a damaged or crumbling stone structure.
    • Shape a stone block into a weapon or tool.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Creating a hidden entrance or secret room in a cave or dungeon.
    • Sculpting a temporary bridge over a chasm or gap.
    • Modifying a natural cave or tunnel system to fit through with ease.
    • Sculpting a defensive barricade or fortification during a siege.
    • Creating a stone shelter or structure in the wilderness.
