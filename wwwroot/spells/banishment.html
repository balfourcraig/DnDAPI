Banishment

  • You attempt to send one creature that you can see within range to another plane of existence. The target must succeed on a charisma saving throw or be banished.
  • If the target is native to the plane of existence you're on, you banish the target to a harmless demiplane. While there, the target is incapacitated. The target remains there until the spell ends, at which point the target reappears in the space it left or in the nearest unoccupied space if that space is occupied.
  • If the target is native to a different plane of existence than the one you're on, the target is banished with a faint popping noise, returning to its home plane. If the spell ends before 1 minute has passed, the target reappears in the space it left or in the nearest unoccupied space if that space is occupied. Otherwise, the target doesn't return.

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 5th level or higher, you can target one additional creature for each slot level above 4th.

General Tips:

  • To remove a troublesome NPC from a town or city
  • To banish a summoned creature that has turned on the party
  • To send an undead or fiendish creature back to its plane of origin
  • To remove a curse or hex on a party member that is affecting their ability to perform in combat
  • To banish a powerful enemy for a temporary amount of time, allowing the party to escape or plan their next move

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Banishing a powerful demon from the mortal plane to protect a town or city
  • Banishing an evil wizard from a battle to shift the odds in favor of the players
  • Banishing a cursed object to undo its negative effects
  • Banishing a group of hostile creatures to escape a dangerous situation
  • Banishing a fellow player character who has been possessed by an evil entity and needs to be separated from the group for safety
