Healing Word

A creature of your choice that you can see within range regains hit points equal to 1d4 + your spellcasting ability modifier. This spell has no effect on undead or constructs.

Spell Rules

  • 1
  • Evocation
  • 1 bonus action
  • 60 feet
  • Instantaneous
  • V
    • Bard
    • Cleric
    • Druid

At Higher Levels

When you cast this spell using a spell slot of 2nd level or higher, the healing increases by 1d4 for each slot level above 1st.

General Tips:

  • During combat to quickly heal a wounded ally
  • To revive a fallen teammate who is unconscious or dying
  • After combat to top off a party member's health before continuing
  • When exploring a dangerous area and anticipating potential injuries
  • During a long rest to help allies recover from injuries and regain hit points

Interesting Scenarios:

  • Healing a downed ally in combat to get them back in the fight
  • Reviving a fellow player character who has fallen unconscious
  • Healing a non-player character who has vital information or resources
  • Bringing a recently deceased character back to life before their permanent death
  • Mending the wounds of party members after a particularly difficult encounter
  • Helping injured civilians in a village, town or city that may have been attacked or ravaged by monsters
  • Curing poison or disease quickly before it can spread or cause too much harm
  • Stabilizing party members who are at risk of dying from bleeding or other injuries
  • Healing NPCs who are important to the party's quest, such as a quest-giving NPC or a powerful ally
