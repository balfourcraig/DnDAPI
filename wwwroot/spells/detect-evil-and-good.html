Detect Evil and Good

  • For the duration, you know if there is an aberration, celestial, elemental, fey, fiend, or undead within 30 feet of you, as well as where the creature is located. Similarly, you know if there is a place or object within 30 feet of you that has been magically consecrated or desecrated.
  • The spell can penetrate most barriers, but it is blocked by 1 foot of stone, 1 inch of common metal, a thin sheet of lead, or 3 feet of wood or dirt.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • When meeting new NPCs to determine if they have any malicious intent
    • When investigating a haunted or cursed location
    • During negotiations with other players or NPCs to sense any hidden agendas
    • When exploring a dark or dangerous dungeon to detect any evil creatures lurking nearby
    • In battle to spot any enemy enchantments or curses

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • When entering a new town or city, use Detect Evil and Good to determine if any malevolent beings are present and posing a threat to the community.
    • In a dungeon or cave system, use Detect Evil and Good to determine if any undead or evil creatures are lurking ahead, waiting to ambush you.
    • When negotiating with a suspicious NPC, use Detect Evil and Good to determine if they have evil intentions or if they can be trusted.
    • In a battle with an unknown foe, cast Detect Evil and Good to determine if your enemy has any particular weaknesses that can be exploited.
    • In a haunted house, use Detect Evil and Good to determine if any ghosts or undead spirits are haunting the area.
