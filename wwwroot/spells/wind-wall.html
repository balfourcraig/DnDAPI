Wind Wall

  • A wall of strong wind rises from the ground at a point you choose within range. You can make the wall up to 50 feet long, 15 feet high, and 1 foot thick. You can shape the wall in any way you choose so long as it makes one continuous path along the ground. The wall lasts for the duration.
  • When the wall appears, each creature within its area must make a strength saving throw. A creature takes 3d8 bludgeoning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
  • The strong wind keeps fog, smoke, and other gases at bay. Small or smaller flying creatures or objects can't pass through the wall. Loose, lightweight materials brought into the wall fly upward. Arrows, bolts, and other ordinary projectiles launched at targets behind the wall are deflected upward and automatically miss. (Boulders hurled by giants or siege engines, and similar projectiles, are unaffected.) Creatures in gaseous form can't pass through it.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Use Wind Wall to deflect incoming projectiles, like arrows or thrown weapons.
    • Create a barrier to protect yourself and your allies from an incoming threat, like a charging enemy or a swarm of insects.
    • Use Wind Wall to control the movement of enemies, pushing them away from valuable NPCs or objects.
    • Use the Wind Wall to create a barrier between yourself and an enemy spellcaster, preventing them from targeting you with spells that require sight.
    • Use Wind Wall to manipulate the battlefield, creating hazards for your enemies by pushing them into traps, environmental hazards, or off cliffs.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Blocking off a narrow path to prevent enemies from passing through
    • Shielding a group of allies from ranged attacks
    • Deflecting incoming projectiles such as arrows or bolts
    • Creating a barrier to protect against strong gusts or winds
    • Concealing the movement or actions of a group by creating a wall of swirling wind

    How do you want to do this?:

    • Whirlwind: The caster summons a fierce whirlwind that slices through their opponents, dealing massive slashing damage.
    • Gale Force: A powerful gust of wind slams into the target, knocking them back several feet and leaving them stunned.
    • Blade Barrier: The Wind Wall becomes razor-sharp, creating a barrier of swirling blades that slices any opponent who dares to approach.
    • Sonic Boom: A deafening sonic boom shatters the air around the target, dealing thunder damage and knocking them prone.
    • Air Blast: The caster unleashes a concentrated blast of wind that sends their opponent flying backwards, dealing force damage.
