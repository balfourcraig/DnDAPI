Hypnotic Pattern

  • You create a twisting pattern of colors that weaves through the air inside a 30-foot cube within range. The pattern appears for a moment and vanishes. Each creature in the area who sees the pattern must make a wisdom saving throw. On a failed save, the creature becomes charmed for the duration. While charmed by this spell, the creature is incapacitated and has a speed of 0.
  • The spell ends for an affected creature if it takes any damage or if someone else uses an action to shake the creature out of its stupor.

    • When facing a large group of enemies, use Hypnotic Pattern to temporarily incapacitate several of them while you deal with the rest.
    • Use the spell to create a distraction, allowing you and your allies to sneak past guards or obstacles unnoticed.
    • When trying to interrogate or negotiate with an enemy, use Hypnotic Pattern to put them in a suggestible state and get more information out of them.
    • Use Hypnotic Pattern to disperse a group of hostile NPCs or creatures, giving you time to think of a plan or escape.
    • If you have a healer or support class in your party, Hypnotic Pattern can be used to create a safe zone where they can tend to wounded allies without being interrupted.
