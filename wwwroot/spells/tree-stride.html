Tree Stride

  • You gain the ability to enter a tree and move from inside it to inside another tree of the same kind within 500 feet. Both trees must be living and at least the same size as you. You must use 5 feet of movement to enter a tree. You instantly know the location of all other trees of the same kind within 500 feet and, as part of the move used to enter the tree, can either pass into one of those trees or step out of the tree you're in. You appear in a spot of your choice within 5 feet of the destination tree, using another 5 feet of movement. If you have no movement left, you appear within 5 feet of the tree you entered.
  • You can use this transportation ability once per round for the duration. You must end each turn outside a tree.

    General Tips:

    • Escape from combat when surrounded.

    • Surprise an enemy by appearing out of a nearby tree.
    • Get to a safe location quickly to rest or hide from danger.
    • Gain a strategic advantage by reaching a high vantage point quickly.

    • Sneaking past enemies by jumping from tree to tree without touching the ground

    • Reaching a high vantage point to scout an area or look for a specific object or creature
    • Surprising enemies by suddenly appearing behind them after teleporting through trees

    • Escaping pursuit by quickly moving through trees that the pursuers can't follow
    • Retrieving a valuable item that has been hidden in a tree or on a tree branch
