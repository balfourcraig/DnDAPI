Ray of Enfeeblement

  • A black beam of enervating energy springs from your finger toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target deals only half damage with weapon attacks that use Strength until the spell ends.
  • At the end of each of the target's turns, it can make a constitution saving throw against the spell. On a success, the spell ends.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Against powerful melee opponents like giants, ogres, or trolls to reduce their strength and damage output.
    • On mages or other spellcasters to weaken their spellcasting abilities and make them easier to take out.
    • During a negotiation or diplomatic encounter to weaken the physical strength and intimidation of an opponent.
    • As a support spell for tanks and fighters, reducing the damage they take by weakening enemy attacks.
    • Against monsters with high Constitution scores like undead or constructs, reducing their overall effectiveness in combat.

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Weakening a powerful enemy before engaging them in combat
    • Reducing the physical strength of a strong opponent to prevent them from escaping
    • Using the spell to weaken an enemy before attempting to negotiate with them
    • Protecting an ally by reducing the strength of their attacker
    • Using the spell on a character who has betrayed the party to render them less of a threat
    • Dealing with a non-lethal threat, such as a rampaging animal or berserker
