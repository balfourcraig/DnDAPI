Nondetection

For the duration, you hide a target that you touch from divination magic. The target can be a willing creature or a place or an object no larger than 10 feet in any dimension. The target can't be targeted by any divination magic or perceived through magical scrying sensors.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • When trying to smuggle contraband past guards or other authorities
    • When sneaking into enemy territory undetected
    • When trying to avoid detection by magical scrying or divination spells
    • When attempting to hide from a powerful enemy or group of enemies
    • When infiltrating a highly secure location or organization

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Evading a powerful enemy's attempts to scry on your movements
    • Infiltrating an enemy stronghold undetected by their magic sensors
    • Stealing an enchanted object without alerting its guardian wards
    • Sneaking past a trap that activates on the detection of magic
    • Hiding from a pursuing enemy who is using magic to track you
