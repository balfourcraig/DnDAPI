Dimension Door

  • You teleport yourself from your current location to any other spot within range. You arrive at exactly the spot desired. It can be a place you can see, one you can visualize, or one you can describe by stating distance and direction, such as "200 feet straight downward" or "upward to the northwest at a 45-degree angle, 300 feet."
  • You can bring along objects as long as their weight doesn't exceed what you can carry. You can also bring one willing creature of your size or smaller who is carrying gear up to its carrying capacity. The creature must be within 5 feet of you when you cast this spell.
  • If you would arrive in a place already occupied by an object or a creature, you and any creature traveling with you each take 4d6 force damage, and the spell fails to teleport you.

At Higher Levels

    General Tips:

    • Sneaking into a locked room or building
    • Escaping danger by teleporting to safety
    • Getting past obstacles or barriers that block your path
    • Quickly traveling to a distant location without having to trek through dangerous terrain
    • Surprising enemies in battle by teleporting behind them or into their midst
    • Rescuing an ally or retrieving an object from a difficult-to-reach location

    Interesting Scenarios:

    • Escaping a dangerous situation by teleporting to safety
    • Surprising enemies by appearing directly behind them in combat
    • Reaching otherwise inaccessible areas by teleporting through barriers
    • Rescuing a fellow player who is trapped or otherwise unable to move
    • Last-minute escapes from areas under siege or imminent attack

    How do you want to do this?:

    • The caster opens a portal to the Astral Plane, causing their opponent to be sucked through and completely disintegrated.
    • The caster teleports above their opponent and drops onto them from a great height, dealing massive force damage.
    • The caster teleports their opponent into a nearby body of water, suffocating them as they struggle to breathe.
    • The caster teleports behind their opponent and quickly slices them with a dagger, before teleporting away before they can react.
    • The caster teleports their opponent into a pit of acid or lava, causing them to dissolve in seconds.
